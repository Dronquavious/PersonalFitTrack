Core Features:

    Workout Logging:

        Log workouts with type (e.g., cardio, strength), duration, and calories burned.
        Categorize workouts (e.g., walking, running, weightlifting).
        Progress Tracking:

        Track metrics like total workouts, calories burned, and duration over time.
    Goal Setting:

        Set fitness goals (e.g., lose 5 pounds, run 10km in 60 minutes).
        Show progress toward each goal.
        Meal Planning and Calorie Tracking:

        Log meals with calorie counts and track daily calorie intake.
        Integrate with external APIs (optional) to fetch nutritional information.
    Reports and Graphs:

        Generate progress reports.
        maybe Visualize data with bar charts, line graphs, or pie charts for easy tracking.
